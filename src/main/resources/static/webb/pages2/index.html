Benefits of having a credit card. Finance your purchases. One of the most important advantages is that you can finance your purchases of products and services. Loans

A personal loan is an amount of money that a lender (a bank, credit union, or state-licensed lender) gives to a borrower (the borrower). The borrower has to repay the loan plus interest and fees, within a certain period of time.
